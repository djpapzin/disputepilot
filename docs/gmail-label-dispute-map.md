## Cross-category intelligence patterns

DisputePilot should recognize these recurring patterns across categories:

- **Deadline pressure without proof** — a response is demanded, but supporting evidence is not supplied.
- **Template response gap** — the company acknowledges the complaint but does not answer the specific disputed points.
- **Reconciliation mismatch** — claimed balance, paid amount, approved refund, or bureau status does not match the timeline.
- **Missing escalation path** — the company response omits regulator/ombud/escalation instructions.
- **Evidence repeat loop** — proof is requested again despite already being supplied.
- **Closure without remedy** — a ticket is closed while the underlying issue remains unresolved.

## Redaction warning for all categories

Before any real-world use, DisputePilot must remove or replace:

- real names and private email addresses;
- phone numbers and physical addresses;
- ID/passport numbers;
- account, policy, claim, ticket, transaction, and case numbers;
- screenshots, statements, invoices, attachments, and reports;
- exact private email wording and quoted correspondence.
